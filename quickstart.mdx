## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account for version control integration

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for Midhun Gk to access your documentation workspace.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and access your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Organize your projects by creating a dedicated space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** in the sidebar and enter a name like `My Project Docs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Permissions" icon="shield">
    Choose public, private, or team access based on your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Description" icon="file-text">
    Write a brief overview, such as `Documentation for my latest app.`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Name your spaces descriptively to easily find them later. Use keywords like project name or version.

</Callout>

## Customize and Add Your First Page

Tailor your space with custom settings and create an initial page using MDX.

First, configure basic options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Theme" icon="palette">
    Select your brand color `#3B82F6` in **Space Settings > Appearance**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Navigation" icon="menu">
    Enable sidebar menu and add a logo under **Space Settings > Layout**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Now, add your first page with a simple MDX example:

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Bash">
  ```mdx
  ---
  title: Welcome
  description: Your first page.
  ---

  ## Hello World

  Welcome to Midhun Gk!
  ```
  ```bash
  # If using CLI (optional)
  mgk space create my-space --page welcome.mdx
  ```
</CodeGroup>

Save and publish to see your page live.
