---
title: Adding New Documentation
description: Guide to create a documentation page in Midhun Gk.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    On the dashboard, select your project from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new page">
    Click the "+ Add Page" button in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Give your page a title and write your documentation in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Hit "Save" or "Publish" to make your doc live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
