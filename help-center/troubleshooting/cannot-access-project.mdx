## Problem
- Project dashboard won't load
- "Access denied" or blank screen

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your login">
    Make sure you are signed in at `https://docs.midhungk.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify permissions">
    Only project members can access private docs. Ask an admin to invite you if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Try logging out, clearing your browser cache, and signing in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
